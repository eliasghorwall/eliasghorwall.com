Swagatam

September 2017

This project included the design and development of a website for a local Indian restaurant. My goal was to take Indian culture and combine it with minimalist design, resulting in a clear user experience whilst still communicating the cultural aspect of the restaurant and its food. A method used for this was the creation of a color scheme for each page by selecting UI colors based on the specific imagery.
